About Zor!

Zor is a group of talented and driven UCSD students who each bring their unique style and experience together to create an exciting mix of Bollywood and modern dance styles.

Recent Notable Achievements:

  • Bollywood America: Best Mix, Best Choreography, & Best Male dancer Awards
  • Chicago Tufaan 2011: 1st place winner
  • Bollywood Berkely 2011: 2nd place winner
  • Dance-Fusion 2010: 1st place winner
  • Jhoomti Shaam 2010: 1st place winner in Fusion
  • Chicago Agni 2009: 1st place winner
  • Jhoomti Shaam 2009: 3rd place winner

Foundation

UCSD's Hindi Film Dance team was created in 2001 in the hopes of promoting South Asian dance awareness at UCSD. Since, the team has been competing all over the nation and has built a reputation for itself as one of the finest fusion Bollywood teams in the nation.
